About Little Lemon

Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant located in the heart of the city. Founded by two brothers with a passion for fresh, vibrant flavors, our mission is to bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your table.

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and preparing each dish with care and love. Our menu features classic Mediterranean recipes with a modern twist, from crisp Greek salads to savory grilled meats and delicious vegetarian options.

At Little Lemon, we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all our guests. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, we want you to feel at home.

– James Beard
